Welcome to the GardeNet Blog!

Blog Update #1

Fri Feb 19 2016 14:36:56 GMT-0500 (Eastern Standard Time)
The GardeNet team has been working hard on updating their project to be fully functional. Currently, they are working on coding for the GardeNet Controller to operate the garden sprinklers. Most of the hardware functionality is finished for operating flow sensors and solenoids that will be dispersed over the garden. The team has successfully achieved wireless functionality on the flow sensors and solenoids using the RF signals. Now focus has shifted to connecting the hardware components to the cloud server via 3G modem.
