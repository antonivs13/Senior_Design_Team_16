Save Water

We save you water by automating the gardening process. With our system, no water goes wasted and scheduling becomes a breeze.

group

Community Focused

By using community gardens as the main focus of GardeNet. Interpersonal connections can be structured through the ease of automation. The stress of forgetting to turn off a watering system is long behind as we create an easy way to control valves in the garden. Leaving you with more time to tend to friends and family.

tap_and_play

Mobile Controlled

At GardeNet we are implementing garden automation through the use of mobile devices. This creates a larger scale IoT platform for which garden data can be collected at the touch of a button.
